French Toast

This fabulous French toast recipe works with many types of bread â€” white, whole wheat, brioche, cinnamon-raisin, Italian, or French! Delicious served hot with butter and maple syrup.

Ingredients

  • White Bread
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Vanilla and cinnamon
  • Salt
  • Butter

Directions

Step 1

Whisk milk, eggs, vanilla, cinnamon, and salt together in a shallow bowl.

Step 2

Lightly butter a griddle or skillet and heat over medium-high heat.

Step 3

Dunk bread in the egg mixture, soaking both sides.

Step 4

Transfer to the hot skillet and cook until golden, 3 to 4 minutes per side. Serve hot.
